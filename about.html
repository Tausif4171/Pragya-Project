About Checkmate

Managed by a team of highly qualified and seasoned professionals, Checkmate Institute is run by M/s Pragya Immigration and Educare Private Ltd. CA Sunil Kumar Jindal is the director of the start-up, who has a varied experience of 20 years as Chartered Accountant. Ms Navita Jindal, Director has been the mentor and chief trainer in the Checkmate institute. She has a knack for identifying and polishing the language capabilities of IELTS-aspirants.

Checkmate understands the learning needs of aspirants who are eager to reach foreign shores after clearing the mandatory English test. In special cases, we strive to aid students to appear in exam early, without compromising the quality of the teaching. Our unique approach helps student to understand modules better and apply curated strategies in exam.

Our teaching methodologies differentiates us from others. Besides the usual teaching, we also focus on various strategies and tips to score higher band. We lay special emphasis on time-management in the exam. For us, every candidate has a unique personality. We spend time on one-to-one counselling to build confidence in the aspirants.
